Who we are and how it all got started

Having worked at various record labels and distributors over the past 6 years, we decided there has to be a better way.

As the industry has changed over the last ten years, moving from a physical-led market to a digital one, we felt that data integrity hadn't grown with this change. So we set out to try and change this, to pass on our knowledge of how data is powering this industry, and how if not dealt with properly money and opportunities can fall through the cracks.

So we created Metable, the catalogue management tool we always wished we had.

Our Beliefs
